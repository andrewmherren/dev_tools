# Brainstorming

Use this skill before implementation starts.

## Procedure

1. Ask focused questions to clarify goals, users, constraints, and non-goals.
2. Propose at least two viable approaches with trade-offs.
3. Recommend one approach with rationale.
4. Produce an approval-ready design summary.

## Output

- Problem statement
- Constraints and assumptions
- Chosen approach and trade-offs
- Clear acceptance criteria
